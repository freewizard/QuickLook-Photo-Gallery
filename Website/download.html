Getting Started

Installation

To install, simply click the "Download Extension" link above and click "Install". If you have the "Open Safe Files" option disabled, double-click the extension in your "Downloads" folder and click "Install".

Usage

After installation, a new button will be added to your toolbar. If any images are available on the page you are viewing, clicking this button will bring up the image gallery.

Preferences

To access preferences for the extension, including minimum size of images to be displayed, image types to be displayed, and length of slides in slideshow mode, click the "Safari" menu and select "Preferences". Go to the "Extensions" tab, and you can manage preferences for all extensions, including QuickLook Photo Gallery. You can also disable or uninstall the extension from this preference pane.

Removal

To remove the extension, visit the Preferences as described above. There you can temporarily disable or permanently uninstall the extension
